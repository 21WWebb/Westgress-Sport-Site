About

Summer at Westgress is a FREE summer program for kids aged 10-16. We want kids to be more active during the holidays, through fun activities and enjoyable experiences. Kids can join regardless of fitness or sporting ability.

Location:

Date & Time:

The Summer at Westgress program will be occuring from August 4th to August 10th from 10am to 3pm. It is advised to pack lunch for your children as no lunch will be available for purchase during the program.

Throughout each day there will be two groups for the activities, one for 10 year olds to 13 year olds and another for 14 year olds to 16 year olds.
